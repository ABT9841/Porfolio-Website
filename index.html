About Me

Hey, I’m Alish—a data analyst with a passion for turning messy data into meaningful insights. My journey has been all about diving deep into data and uncovering the stories it tells.

In my Bellabeat Smart Device Usage Analysis project, I analyzed over 100,000 rows of Fitbit data to reveal key trends and better understand user behavior. I’ve also refined a complex layoffs dataset—cleaning thousands of records and building dynamic Power BI dashboards—to highlight critical trends in the tech industry. Additionally, my Travel Analysis Segmentation project involved using clustering techniques with Python to uncover distinct customer segments, paving the way for targeted marketing strategies.

Leveraging skills in SQL, Python, Excel, Tableau, and Power BI, along with my Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate, I approach each project with a hands-on, collaborative spirit. Transforming raw numbers into strategic insights, I’m committed to making data work for real-world impact.
